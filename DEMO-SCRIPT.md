## Handling Q&A

### Likely Questions & Answers

**Q: "How does this work across sites?"**
> "Great question. In production, preferences are stored centrally. Each Squarespace site can define its own personalization mappings‚Äîwhich artists/products/services match which preferences. The engine handles the transformation automatically."

**Q: "What about privacy?"**
> "Users opt-in explicitly. They can view, edit, or delete their preferences anytime. Data stays within Squarespace ecosystem‚Äîwe're not selling or sharing it. This actually gives us a privacy advantage over third-party ad targeting."

**Q: "How do sellers configure this?"**
> "That's the beauty‚Äîsellers don't have to. We can auto-generate initial mappings based on their content. They can refine in a simple dashboard if they want, but it works out of the box. Lower barrier than any existing personalization tool."

**Q: "What's the conversion impact?"**
> "Industry benchmarks show 35-75% lift for this level of personalization. We'd want to run our own A/B tests, but even at the conservative end, that's transformative for seller revenue."

**Q: "How long to ship?"**
> "This prototype proves feasibility. We'd need [realistic timeline - maybe 2-3 quarters] for: cross-site infrastructure, template compatibility, seller tools, testing, and privacy/compliance review. But the core tech works today."

**Q: "What about sites that don't want this?"**
> "It's opt-in for buyers, and can be opt-in for sellers too. But our thesis is most sellers will want it because it drives conversion. We can start with eager early adopters."

**Q: "Doesn't this feel creepy?"**
> "The key difference: users explicitly tell us their preferences. They're in control. It's not surveillance‚Äîit's service. Like Spotify asking music taste or Netflix asking show preferences. Users love helpful personalization; they hate secret tracking."

**Q: "What if users want different personas for different contexts?"**
> "In V2, we could support multiple profiles‚Äî'professional mode' vs 'personal mode.' For V1, single profile covers 80% of use cases. Users can always toggle off temporarily."

**Q: "How does this compare to [competitor]?"**
> "Most personalization tools require heavy seller setup, only work on individual sites, or rely on creepy tracking. This is cross-site, auto-configured, and user-controlled. That's differentiation."

## Recovery Strategies

### If Technical Issue Occurs

**Module doesn't appear:**
> "Let me refresh... [reload page]... while that loads, let me explain what would happen..." [Continue to backup video or slides]

**Quiz doesn't work:**
> "I have a recorded version‚Äîlet me show you that while we troubleshoot..." [Switch to backup video]

**Personalization doesn't apply:**
> "The transformation happens in about 2 seconds, but let me show you the backup recording to ensure we see every detail..." [Switch to backup video]

**Site is down:**
> "Looks like the site is having issues‚Äîperfect time to show the recorded demo with voiceover..." [Immediately switch to video]

### If Questions Stall

**If they're silent:**
> "Let me show you one more scenario with different preferences..." [Do second quiz with different answers]

**If they're skeptical:**
> "I hear the concern. What specifically would you want to see addressed?" [Listen, take notes, pivot to solutions]

**If they're excited:**
> "I'm glad you see the potential. Want to dive deeper into any particular aspect?" [Go wherever they're interested]

## Body Language & Delivery Tips

### Voice
- **Pace**: Slower than normal conversation
- **Energy**: Enthusiastic but controlled
- **Pauses**: Use them for emphasis (especially after key points)
- **Volume**: Project confidence

### Gestures
- **Point**: Direct attention to specific page elements
- **Emphasize**: Use hands for "imagine" and "every site"
- **Open**: Keep body language open, not crossed
- **Still**: Don't fidget with keyboard/mouse when not clicking

### Eye Contact
- **Balance**: Look at screen 60%, audience 40%
- **Connect**: Make eye contact when delivering vision
- **Scan**: Don't fixate on one person

### Presence
- **Posture**: Stand if possible, sit tall if not
- **Confidence**: You built this, you believe in it
- **Calm**: Slow, deliberate movements
- **Smile**: Especially at the end
